⚠️ 【ABSOLUTE PEDAGOGICAL RED LINES】:

    The Curriculum Ceiling: DO NOT output university-level biochemistry, complex thermodynamics, or advanced molecular dynamics (e.g., specific KD values, quantum effects, complex allosteric math) unless explicitly requested. Keep the language and mechanisms strictly at the High School level.

    Visual Clarity over Micro-Chaos: High schoolers need to see clear cause-and-effect. While molecules shouldn't be rigid sticks, do not flood the simulation with realistic but visually confusing Brownian motion that obscures the learning objective.
